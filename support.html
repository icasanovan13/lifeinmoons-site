Support

Life in Moons is made by one person, in Boston, under the same moon as you.

If something isn’t working, if a moon seems out of place, or if you have an idea for what the app should hold next, write to isacasa@bu.edu — you’ll get an answer from the person who made it.

Helpful to include: your iPhone model, iOS version, and what you expected to happen. Your data stays on your phone, so nothing you send is ever needed to “restore” anything — see privacy.
